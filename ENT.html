Our ENT Services

Comprehensive Diagnostics

Out ENT department provides a full range of clinical services for the diagnosis and treatment of both simple and complicated diseases of the ear, nose, throat in children and adults. We are the only private healthcare providers in Jaffna offering a variety of ENT surgeries with stat of the art technology with minimized post operative pain and other complications.

Procedures & Treatments

Hearing Tests

Advanced audiometry and balance assessments for accurate hearing health evaluations.

Nasal & Sinus Surgery

Minimally invasive procedures to treat chronic sinusitis, polyps, and nasal obstructions.

Throat Treatments

Effective management of tonsillitis, vocal cord disorders, and other throat conditions.
